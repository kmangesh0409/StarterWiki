Ms Dhoni


FULL NAME : Mahendra Singh Dhoni
PLACE OF BIRTH : Ranchi, Jharkhand, (Then in Bihar) India
BORN : 7 July 1981
HEIGHT : 5 ft 9 in (175 cm)
JERSEY NO. : 7
BATTING STYLE : Right-Handed
ROLE : Wicketkeeper-Batsman
TEAM :India
IPL Chennai Super Kings

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, known as MS Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer who has achieved numerous records and honors during his career. He is the first Indian wicket-keeper to score 4,000 Test runs, and he holds the record for the most dismissals in an innings and career by an Indian wicket-keeper. Dhoni has also won major tournaments and holds several records as a batsman and wicket-keeper. He has received various awards and honors, including the Padma Bhushan and the ICC ODI Player of the Year. Dhoni has also faced controversy, including a tax evasion controversy and a spot-fixing scandal. He has played the most international matches as captain and has a net worth of approximately INR 1030 crore.
